## Assignment 3: Experimentation and Expansion

---

## Objective

Encourage **original research thinking** by extending the reproduced work with new experiments.

Students should **build on insights from Assignment 1 and 2**.

---

## Tasks

### 1. Research Proposal

Propose **one or more improvements**, such as:

- Model architecture modification

- New loss function

- Additional training data

- Cross-domain evaluation

- Efficiency improvements

- Multimodal extension

---

### 2. Experimental Design

Students must clearly define:

- Hypothesis

- Experimental setup

- Baselines

- Evaluation metrics

---

### 3. Conduct Experiments

Run experiments and compare:

- Original model

- Reproduced baseline

- Proposed method (Optional Bonus) or Atleast one More Addional Dataset (Must)

---

### 4. Results & Analysis

Include:

- Performance comparison tables

- Visualizations (graphs, confusion matrices)

- Statistical significance tests (if applicable)

---

### 5. Research Discussion

Explain:

- What improvements were achieved

- Why the modification works (or fails)

- Limitations of the proposed approach

---

## Deliverables

**Report Length:** 10–12 pages

Structure:

- Introduction

- Background and Paper Summary

- Reproduction Summary

- Proposed Method

- Experimental Setup

- Results and Analysis

- Discussion

- Conclusion

- References

Students must also submit:

- **Updated code repository**

- **Experiment documentation**
